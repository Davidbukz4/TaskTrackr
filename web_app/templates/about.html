About Us

This is a Portfolio Project from Holberton School, created by David Egwutu and Esther Ekanem. TaskTrackr was born out of our shared passion for helping users stay organized with their daily lives and activities. We were inspired to create an app that would enhance users' organizational capabilities and empower them to manage their tasks effectively.
